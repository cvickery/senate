Technology Fee Task Force Application

One of the functions of the Academic Senate’s Technology and Library Committee is to recommend faculty and students to serve on the college’s Technology Fee Task Force. The Technology Fee Task Force is responsible for deciding how to spend the college’s share of the university’s technology fee, currently $75 per full-time student.
