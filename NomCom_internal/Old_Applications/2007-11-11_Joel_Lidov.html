Statement:

I have served on the committee during the development of the new General Education requirements and I have been actively involved in this task. Now that we have have reached the implementation stage for the new PLAS requirements, I look forward to continuing that work as we see new or revised course through the process of entering the curriculum. I think it is particularly important that we keep our focus on the goals of undergraduate liberal arts as a comprehensive preparation for a thoughtful and productive life, and that we base our decisions on the best reflections of the students and faculty. So I remain especially interested in how general education is integrated into the other aspects of undergraduate education, and also, as representative of the Arts Division, in the role of the programs in the Arts and Humanities both as a group of majors and as contributors to the intellectual life of the College.
